Mission Briefing

For generations, the Star Force Academy has trained the finest pilots in the galaxy.

Located on the distant orbital station Astra Prime, the Academy exists for one purpose: to prepare elite starfighter pilots to defend humanity's outer colonies. Only a handful of cadets ever graduate from its rigorous training program.

You are one of them.

Your final test was meant to be simple — a solo patrol beyond the outer asteroid belt. A routine exercise designed to prove you were ready to wear the Star Force insignia.

But the patrol zone was not empty.

Unidentified enemy fleets have begun emerging from deep space, appearing suddenly across multiple sectors. Their ships move in organized formations and attack anything that enters their territory.

No one knows where they came from.
No one knows how many there are.

With communication relays failing and reinforcements hours away, your ship is the only defense standing between the enemy fleet and the nearby civilian colonies.

Star Force Command has issued a single directive:

Hold the line.

Your mission is not to conquer the enemy fleet.
Your mission is to survive as long as possible, destroying enemy forces and buying precious time for the colonies to evacuate and for Star Force reinforcements to arrive.

Every enemy ship destroyed delays the invasion.
Every second you survive protects millions.

You are no longer a cadet.

You are now a Star Force Pilot.

Engines ready.
Weapons hot.

The battle begins now.
