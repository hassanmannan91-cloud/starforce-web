STAR FORCE: COSMIC RUN

OFFICIAL ARCADE PILOT HANDBOOK

WELCOME, PILOT

The galaxy is in danger.

Enemy fleets have begun invading deep space sectors, sending wave after wave of hostile ships across the stars. Only the elite pilots of Star Force can hold the line.

You are one of those pilots.

Climb into the cockpit of your starfighter and prepare for combat. Dodge incoming fire, destroy enemy formations, collect valuable coins, and push your reflexes to their limits.

The longer you survive, the more dangerous the battlefield becomes.

Good luck, pilot. The galaxy is counting on you.

⸻

GAME OBJECTIVE

Your mission is simple:

Survive as long as possible while destroying enemy forces.

During each run you will:

  • Battle waves of enemy ships
  • Dodge incoming projectiles
  • Collect coins scattered across the battlefield
  • Increase your score
  • Survive escalating enemy attacks

The longer you stay alive, the more intense the enemy assault becomes.

Only the best pilots achieve legendary high scores.

⸻

ENEMY FORCES

Enemy fleets consist of five classes of alien ships, each with their own combat behavior.

Type 1 — Scout Fighters

Light enemy ships that appear frequently in early waves.

These fighters travel in organized formations and occasionally fire simple projectiles toward the player.

Do not underestimate them—large formations can quickly overwhelm an unprepared pilot.

Type 2 — Assault Ships

These enemies move faster and often appear in coordinated patterns.

Expect sudden directional movements and aggressive formations designed to trap the player.

Type 3 — Attack Interceptors

After the player destroys 20 enemies, these ships unlock their firing systems.

Once activated, Type 3 enemies fire projectiles more frequently, creating dangerous attack zones.

Type 4 — Missile Carriers

After 30 enemy kills, Type 4 enemies activate their most dangerous weapon.

Homing missiles.

These missiles track the player's ship and require quick movement and sharp reflexes to avoid.

Late in the game, Missile Carriers become one of the most dangerous threats in space.

Type 5 — Heavy Assault Waves

Type 5 enemies appear in large sweeping formations.

These waves often travel horizontally across the battlefield, entering from either side of the screen.

Their size and movement patterns can suddenly fill the entire combat zone.

Stay alert when these waves appear.

ENEMY FORMATIONS

Enemy fleets attack in many formation patterns, including:

  • Single attackers
  • Horizontal lines
  • Vertical columns
  • Square blocks
  • V-shaped formations
  • Complex tactical patterns

Because formations are randomly generated, no two runs are exactly the same.

Expect the unexpected.

ENEMY MOVEMENT

Enemy ships use various movement styles during battle:

  • Straight descent attacks
  • Slow drifting motion
  • Diagonal sweeps
  • Horizontal fly-bys
  • Screen-wide sweeping movements

Each movement pattern requires different positioning and timing from the player.

⸻

PLAYER SYSTEMS

Starfighter Controls

As the pilot, you must guide your starfighter through enemy territory.

Use quick movements to:

  • Avoid enemy ships
  • Dodge incoming projectiles
  • Position for clean attacks
  • Collect coins

Mastery of movement is the key to survival.

Weapon System & Ammo

Your starfighter is equipped with a powerful weapon system, but ammunition must be managed carefully.

The HUD displays an ammo bar that tracks remaining firepower.

Ammo levels are color-coded:

  • Green — Full ammo
  • Yellow — Medium ammo
  • Red — Low ammo

When ammunition becomes critically low, the bar may pulse to warn the player.

Use your shots wisely.

HEADS-UP DISPLAY (HUD)

During gameplay, the HUD provides essential battle information.

The display shows:

  • Coin count
  • Ammo status
  • Score

Monitoring the HUD can mean the difference between victory and destruction.

⸻

COINS & SCORING

Coins

Coins appear during gameplay and can be collected by flying through them.

Coins may be used to unlock ships and other upgrades.

Skilled pilots collect as many coins as possible during each run.

Score

Your score increases when you:

  • Destroy enemy ships
  • Survive longer
  • Maintain control during intense waves

Challenge yourself to beat your previous high score.

⸻

THE DIFFICULTY SYSTEM

Star Force: Cosmic Run features a dynamic difficulty system designed to constantly increase the challenge.

Ramp Cycle

Every 10 seconds, the battlefield alternates between two phases:

Ramp ON
Enemy spawn activity increases.

Ramp OFF
Enemy spawn pressure temporarily decreases.

This cycle keeps the battle unpredictable.

Global Difficulty Increase

Every minute you survive, the game increases:

  • Enemy movement speed
  • Enemy firing rate
  • Enemy spawn frequency

At peak intensity, enemies may operate at three times their original strength.

Only the most skilled pilots survive deep into the run.

⸻

SPECIAL FEATURES

Hidden Clock Mode

A mysterious clock button appears on the Start Screen.

Activating it reveals a special alternate mode.

Curious pilots may wish to experiment and discover what it does.

GAME FLOW

Starting the Mission

  1. Launch the game
  2. The Start Screen appears with an animated starfield background
  3. Press Start
  4. Select your ship
  5. Enter combat

Combat Loop

During gameplay:

  1. Enemy waves spawn in random formations
  2. Enemies move and fire at the player
  3. The player dodges and attacks
  4. Coins appear and can be collected
  5. Difficulty increases the longer you survive

The battle continues until the player's ship is destroyed.

GAME OVER

Your mission ends when your starfighter collides with:

  • An enemy ship
  • An enemy projectile

After defeat, your score is recorded.

Try again and push further into enemy territory.
